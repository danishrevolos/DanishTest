If you are required to charge tax on the sale of a contract, please enter the tax rate above. By entering the tax rate, MILO will calculate tax within the quote results if you designate "YES" under "Include in Quote". Tax cannot be included in the Contract Purchase Price section of the coverage Application Page.

It is the responsibility of the seller to know the appropriate tax rate and handling for their state, county, etc.
